Google AI Overviews vs Perplexity: Who Cites What? (We Checked 23 Outputs)

Updated July 2026 By Reviewly 8 min read

Google AI Overviews and Perplexity use fundamentally different citation logic. Google weights structured data, schema markup, and established domain authority. Perplexity weights real-time retrieval, community corroboration, and source diversity. A strategy that works for one will not automatically work for the other.

The Methodology

We ran 23 identical service queries through both Google AI Overviews and Perplexity AI, recording which businesses were cited, which sources were referenced, and what content format was used in each citation. Queries covered service categories including legal, medical, home services, financial advice, and professional services, all with an Australian location modifier.

Google AI Overviews: What Gets Cited

Google AI Overviews heavily favours content from established domains with strong structured data. In 19 of 23 queries, the cited businesses had complete schema markup including LocalBusiness, FAQPage, or Review schema. In 21 of 23 queries, the cited content was from pages that already ranked in the top 10 organic results for the query.

The practical implication: Google AI Overviews is an extension of traditional SEO. If you rank well organically and have complete schema, you are likely to appear in AI Overviews. If you do not rank organically, AI Overviews will not rescue you.

Perplexity: What Gets Cited

Perplexity showed a completely different pattern. In 17 of 23 queries, Perplexity cited sources that did not appear in Google's top 10 organic results. Perplexity heavily weighted Reddit threads, Quora answers, industry forums, and review aggregators.

Perplexity also showed a strong preference for source diversity. Rather than citing one authoritative source, it typically cited 4-6 sources across different platform types. This means a business with a strong presence across multiple independent platforms is more likely to appear in Perplexity than a business with a single authoritative website.

The Strategic Implication

Businesses that rely solely on traditional SEO will perform well in Google AI Overviews but poorly in Perplexity. Businesses that focus only on review and community platform presence will perform well in Perplexity but may miss Google AI Overviews.

The optimal strategy addresses both systems simultaneously. This is why the REVIEW Method builds signals across both structured data (for Google) and distributed community platforms (for Perplexity).

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Google AI Overviews use the same sources as regular Google search?

Largely yes. Google AI Overviews heavily favours content that already ranks in the top 10 organic results. Strong schema markup and established domain authority are the primary citation predictors.

How does Perplexity decide what to cite?

Perplexity uses real-time retrieval and weights source diversity. Reddit, Quora, industry forums, and review aggregators are frequently cited alongside traditional websites. Community corroboration is a strong signal.

Should I optimise for Google AI Overviews or Perplexity?

Both. The signals required for each system are complementary. Schema markup and organic rankings help with Google AI Overviews. Distributed platform presence and community mentions help with Perplexity.
