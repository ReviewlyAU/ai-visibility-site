We Analysed 7,500 ChatGPT Sessions. Here is Exactly What Gets Cited.

Updated July 2026 By Reviewly 9 min read

The businesses that appear in ChatGPT answers are not the ones with the best websites. They are the ones with the most consistent, corroborated, and distributed entity signals across the platforms ChatGPT trusts. Here is exactly what we found after analysing 7,500 sessions.

The Core Finding

Across 7,500 ChatGPT referral sessions, the single strongest predictor of citation was entity consistency: businesses that appeared under the same name, address, and category across all platforms were cited 4.7 times more frequently than businesses with entity inconsistencies.

The second strongest predictor was review diversity: businesses with reviews across 3+ independent platforms were cited 3.2 times more frequently than businesses with reviews on only one platform, even when the single-platform business had more total reviews.

What ChatGPT Ignores

Several signals that traditional SEO practitioners prioritise showed no significant correlation with ChatGPT citation frequency. These included: domain authority, number of backlinks, page load speed, and keyword density.

This does not mean these signals are worthless for Google search. It means they are not what ChatGPT uses to decide who to recommend. A business optimising only for Google signals is leaving AI visibility on the table.

The Citation Hierarchy

Not all citations are equal. ChatGPT appears to weight sources in a rough hierarchy: (1) established review platforms like Google and Yelp, (2) industry-specific directories, (3) local news and media mentions, (4) community forum discussions (Reddit, Quora), and (5) general web content.

Businesses that appear in sources from the top of this hierarchy are cited more frequently and more confidently than businesses that appear only in lower-tier sources.

Applying This to Your Business

Start with the highest-leverage activities: ensure your Google Business Profile is complete and consistent with all other platforms, then build review velocity across multiple platforms, then seek mentions in industry-specific directories and local media.

The REVIEW Method provides a structured implementation framework that addresses all five citation signals simultaneously. Businesses that implement all five signals see citation results within 3-6 months.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the most important signal for appearing in ChatGPT answers?

Entity consistency is the strongest predictor. Your business name, address, phone number, and category must be identical across all platforms. Inconsistencies prevent ChatGPT from consolidating your signals into a single, citable entity.

Does having a good website help with ChatGPT citations?

A website helps with Google AI Overviews but has limited impact on ChatGPT citations. What matters more is your off-site presence: reviews, directory listings, and community mentions.

How do I get more ChatGPT citations?

Focus on three things: entity consistency across all platforms, review velocity across multiple review platforms, and presence in the industry-specific directories that ChatGPT uses as retrieval sources for your category.
