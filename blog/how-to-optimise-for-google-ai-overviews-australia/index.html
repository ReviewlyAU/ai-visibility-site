How to Optimise for Google AI Overviews (Before Your Competitors Do)

Updated July 2026 By Reviewly 8 min read

Google AI Overviews appear for the majority of commercial queries in Australia and push organic results below the fold. Being cited in an AI Overview captures the query before the user reaches the organic results. Here is the exact structure required to get cited.

How Google AI Overviews Select Citations

Google AI Overviews draw primarily from content that already ranks in the top 10 organic results for the query. This means AI Overview optimisation starts with traditional SEO: if you do not rank organically, you will not appear in AI Overviews.

However, ranking is necessary but not sufficient. Google also applies a quality filter that favours content with: clear answer capsules in the first 100 words, FAQPage schema markup, structured data (LocalBusiness, Article, or HowTo schema as appropriate), and a high E-E-A-T score.

The Answer Capsule Structure

The most important structural change you can make to improve AI Overview citation is to add a clear answer capsule to the top of every page. An answer capsule is a 2-4 sentence paragraph that directly answers the primary question the page addresses, placed before any other content.

Google's AI Overview system extracts answer capsules and uses them as the basis for generated responses. A page without a clear answer capsule at the top is less likely to be cited than a page with one, even if the rest of the content is superior.

The answer capsule should: start with a direct answer to the query, include the key entity (your business name or service category), be factually specific, and be under 60 words.

Schema Markup for AI Overviews

FAQPage schema is the single most effective schema type for AI Overview citation. Add FAQPage schema to any page that contains question-and-answer content. Structure the schema with the exact questions your target customers ask, and provide complete, specific answers.

LocalBusiness schema is essential for local service businesses. Ensure your schema includes: name, address, phone, opening hours, service area, and service categories. Inconsistencies between your schema and your Google Business Profile will reduce your AI Overview citation probability.

E-E-A-T Signals for AI Overviews

Google's AI Overview system heavily weights E-E-A-T signals. For Australian service businesses, the most important E-E-A-T signals are: professional credentials and certifications (displayed prominently on your website), specific case studies and project examples, author bios with verifiable credentials, and external citations from authoritative sources.

A business that displays its trade licence number, lists specific completed projects with outcomes, and has been mentioned in local media will consistently outperform a business with better content but weaker E-E-A-T signals in AI Overview citations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to rank on page 1 to appear in Google AI Overviews?

Largely yes. Google AI Overviews primarily cite content from the top 10 organic results. Improving your organic rankings is a prerequisite for AI Overview citation.

What schema markup is most important for Google AI Overviews?

FAQPage schema is the most effective for AI Overview citation. LocalBusiness schema is essential for local service businesses. Ensure your schema is consistent with your Google Business Profile data.

How long does it take to appear in Google AI Overviews?

If you already rank in the top 10 organically, adding answer capsules and FAQPage schema can produce AI Overview citations within 2-4 weeks. If you do not rank organically, improving your rankings is the prerequisite step.
