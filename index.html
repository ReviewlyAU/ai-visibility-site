What is Answer Engine Optimisation (AEO)?

AEO is the discipline of structuring your content, entity data, and brand signals so that AI systems like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews understand, trust, and recommend your business.

🔍

Traditional SEO

Optimises a single page to rank in a retrieved list of links. Relies on backlinks and keyword density. Increasingly less effective as AI search grows.

🧠

Answer Engine Optimisation

Builds a distributed, corroborated information architecture across multiple platforms so AI systems can reconstruct and generate answers citing your brand.

🎯

The Result

When someone asks ChatGPT "who should I use for [your service] in Australia?", your business is the answer. Not your competitor.

The REVIEW Method®

Reviewly's proprietary framework for building the five signals that make AI systems trust and cite your brand.

R — Recognised

Your brand name appears consistently across authoritative platforms. AI systems can identify you as a distinct entity, not just a generic service.

E — Established

You have a documented history. Reviews, mentions, and content that show you have been operating and delivering results over time.

V — Verified

Independent sources corroborate your claims. Not just your own website, but third-party directories, publications, and community platforms.

I — Influential

You are cited by sources that AI systems already trust. Reddit threads, industry publications, and authoritative directories that appear in training data.

E — Enduring

Your signals are consistent over time. AI systems favour brands with stable, long-term presence over those with sudden spikes of activity.

W — Worth Choosing

The outcome. When all five signals are present, AI systems have enough evidence to confidently recommend your business over competitors.
